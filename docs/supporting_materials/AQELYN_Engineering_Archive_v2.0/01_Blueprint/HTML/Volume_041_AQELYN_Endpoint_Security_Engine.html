PS-ADR-0036
Continuous Endpoint Assurance
Status: Accepted
Decision
The  Endpoint  Security  Engine  shall  continuously  collect  evidence,  detect  meaningful
configuration  changes,  maintain  verified  baselines,  and  provide  transparent
recommendations without relying on opaque scoring or unexplained conclusions.
Rationale
Endpoints  change  constantly.  Continuous,  evidence-based  observation  provides  more
reliable operational awareness than periodic point-in-time scans alone.

A new differentiator
I would like to introduce the AQELYN Explain Engine.
Every recommendation—whether for a home user or a SOC analyst—should answer five
questions:
1.  What changed?
2.  Why does it matter?
3.  How confident are we?
4.  What evidence supports this conclusion?
5.  What are the recommended next steps?
This  aligns  with  our  goals  of  being  easy  to  use,  WCAG-compliant,  multilingual,
explainable,  and  suitable  for  everyone  from  home  users  to  banks  and
government agencies.
